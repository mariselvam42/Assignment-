Department of Computer science and Engineering

  • Since 1998, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering has operated with success.

  • The program provides both a B.E. and an M.E. in computer science and engineering.

  • The MoUs that the Department has signed are with ORACLE, MICROSOFT (Campus Agreement), Infosys (Campus Connect), and Cisco.

  • For the benefit of faculty and students from different technical educational institutions, research scholars from research institutes, and businesses across India, it arranges symposia, exhibitions, conferences, seminars, and workshops. Since 2009, the Department has held NBA and AICTE accreditation.

  • The department consists of seven air-conditioned, centralized computer centers with 300 systems each, modern computing facilities, and an adequate backup power supply. Top multinational corporations (MNCs) including CTS, TCS, Wipro, Infosys, IBM, Tech Mahendra, IBM, EDS, SAP Labs, Accenture, and others place our students.
